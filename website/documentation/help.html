Add your images

Add your image as background image

You will notice that lot of images are added as background image inside HTML files. To change those images path to your own, just add it to the highlighted part below:


<div class="hero hero--xl u-bg-p-bottom" style="background-image: url('assets/img/home/home-hero.jpg');">

Notice in the code above class:

u-bg-p-bottom

It is helper class that tells element to mainly show the bottom part of the image. Since background images act differently, and not entire part of image is shown, you might need to use different positions to achieve look you want. If you need your image to show center part, use class:

u-bg-p-center

If you want to show mainly top part use:

u-bg-p-top

Add your image inside <img > tag

To add path of your image just replace marked code with your path:

Example: <img src="assets/img/home/story-img.jpg" />

Set your e-mail to Contact form

2. Go to line 11 and add your e-mail.

$to = 'support@themediv.com'; // Write your e-mail here

Add your social profiles

In all HTML files scroll down to Footer section and find following code:

						
<ul class="footer-widget__social-list">
	<!-- Replace # with your Instagram profile URL -->
	<li><a href="#"><ion-icon name="logo-instagram" class="icon icon--grey"></ion-icon></a></li>
	<!-- Replace # with your Facebook profile URL -->
	<li><a href="#"><ion-icon name="logo-facebook" class="icon icon--grey"></ion-icon></a></li>
	<!-- Replace # with your Twitter profile URL -->
	<li><a href="#"><ion-icon name="logo-twitter" class="icon icon--grey"></ion-icon></a></li>
</ul>

Use Ion Icons

Ion icons documentation: https://ionicons.com/

Example of usage:

<ion-icon name="logo-instagram" class="icon icon--grey"></ion-icon>

Change colour scheme

Open style.css located inside assets/css folder and on Line 8, just above table of content you will find:

Primary color: #dcb36e
Secondary color: #faf8f1
Article border color: #f3edde

In entire file find all occurrences of either/and #dcb36e, #faf8f1 and replace with your desired colour in HEX format.
